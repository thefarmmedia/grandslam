— About Us

The Best Things
To Do in Branson

Looking for top-rated indoor entertainment in Branson, Missouri? Grand Slam Entertainment at Ballparks of America is home to three world-class attractions.

Glow Golf Branson MO

Grand Slam Glow Mini Golf features 18 holes of immersive blacklight mini golf under stunning UV lights and neon murals. It's the best glow-in-the-dark mini golf in the Branson area — fun for families, couples, and everyone in between. Each hole brings a new themed challenge and visual surprise.

Zombie Laser Tag in Branson

Operation Outbreak is Branson's most thrilling laser tag experience. Set in a fully themed zombie apocalypse arena, players battle through intense missions with state-of-the-art equipment and immersive special effects. The most unique laser tag in Southwest Missouri — great for groups of all ages.

Escape Rooms Near Branson MO

The Skeleton Key Escape Rooms challenge your mind, your teamwork, and your nerve. With fully immersive themed room experiences, The Skeleton Key is a must-do Branson attraction. Perfect for families, corporate groups, and adventurous couples looking for something truly different.

Indoor Family Fun Branson

Grand Slam Entertainment is Branson's premier indoor family entertainment complex. Located at Ballparks of America, our Triple Play bundle — all three attractions for just $35 — is the best value in Branson for families, tourists, and groups looking for unforgettable fun indoors.
